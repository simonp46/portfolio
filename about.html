I started my studies in software development in 2019 at the institution IUDigital de Antioquia, motivated by my interest in artificial intelligence and process automation. In 2021, I obtained an apprenticeship contract with OneLink and CESDE, where I did my internship for one year. Shortly before the end of this period, I received a job offer from the same company to join as a Jr. software developer, a position in which I worked for two years. During that time, I worked on several projects for clients such as “Avianca”, “LifeMiles”, “Puntos Colombia”, “Zwift”, “Volaris”, “Aceros Guatemala”, among others.

Throughout these projects, I gained experience in technologies such as C#, .NET, Framecoreapp, SQL Server, JavaScript/TypeScript, Angular, React Native, Single SPA and Dialogflow ES-CX, among others. In addition, I developed skills in the creation, configuration and administration of resources in Azure, such as CDN (Content Delivery Network), Azure Functions, AppServices, Service Bus, API Management, Key Vault, using tools such as Azure Storage Explorer for their management.

I also have experience working with agile methodologies like Scrum and Kanban, using tools like Jira for project management. I have worked in collaborative environments with Azure DevOps, where I managed code, configured environments and branch policies, and automated CI/CD processes, including integrations for analytics with SonarCloud.

My professional vision is driven by continuous self-study and a desire to work alongside competent professionals who will help me develop my skills. I aspire to share my knowledge with others, supporting their growth, and, in the long term, to become a software architect, contributing to the design and leadership of high-impact technology solutions.
